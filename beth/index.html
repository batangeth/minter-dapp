About

Batang ETH, also known as BETH, is a project initiated to showcase Filipino culture through the art of NFT. The project's name was derived from the Filipino word "bata," which means "kid," and ETH from the short form of Ethereum — a decentralized, open-source blockchain with smart contract functionality. Its first collection supply consists of 5000 generative Batang ETH NFT that are drawn by the inspiration of the great artwork of Azuki. Moreover, the project aims to give children with cancer in the Philippines the help, hope, and heart to live.

BETH is created by four ambitious Filipinos who use so-called non-fungible tokens (NFTs) not just to collect and sell digital art on the internet but also with the dream of helping the Filipino community someday. The team behind this project is Founder Nate and his colleagues and partners, namely, Kevin, Jinju, and AstroKikko.

Mission

“Create and Craft"

To discover, develop, and deliver innovative art that helps artists hone their knowledge and skill, which can bridge them to the metaverse.

Vision

“Connect and Empowerment”

Our vision is a world where local artists and NFT collectors come together to support and build rapport with each other. We have the power to further develop and introduce Filipino art to the world.

Roadmap

  • Kabanata I

    “Community building and Social Media Strengthening”

    Batang ETH was founded few weeks ago. BETH is focused on community building and is actively searching for NFT creators and collectors. BETH envisions a community where creators and collectors can coincide and help each other.

  • Kabanata II

    “Partnerships and Collaborations”

    Before the launch Batang ETH, BETH aims for partnerships and collaborations with brands, and other NFT projects – especially, the Filipino-lead ones, to support each other and grow the community.

  • Kabanata III

    “Official Website Launching”

    Batang ETH aims to deliver a seamless minting experience for everyone. The developer will check for errors that may occur while having multiple addresses minting and adjust it accordingly. This way, BETH can assure a 100% error-free minting for Batang ETH

  • Kabanata IV

    “Ask the Team Anything”

    Batang ETH aims to share the mission and vision of the project. Core team will answer all the questions of the community.

  • Kabanata V

    “Launching of Batang ETH”

    Minting will start on Batang ETH website. BETH will make every effort to ensure that the minting process will be fair and smooth.

     

    1. Kababata (OG Role) will be minting on the first day.

    2. Kalaro (Whitelist Role) will be minting on the second day.

    3. Public minting will be opened on the third day. Minting is open for a month.

  • Kabanata VI

    “NFT Reveal and Ranking of Batang ETH NFT”

    The reveal of Batang ETH NFT and ranking using our own ranking website.

  • Kabanata VII

    “Giving back to the Community”

    Batang ETH will make sure that the support of the community will not be wasted. BETH will open raffle promos and games for the holders and community that everyone will enjoy. Community is what makes us great.

  • Kabanata VIII

    “Future Development of Batang ETH”

    Other details are to be announced.
