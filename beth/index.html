About

Batang ETH, also known as BETH, is a project created to showcase Filipino culture through the art of NFT-revolving around the whole concept of art in Filipino culture, and more derived from Pinoy work. Batang ETH’s name was derived from the word “bata” means “kid” in English, and ETH from Ethereum, a decentralized, open-source blockchain with smart contract functionality. Batang ETH was founded by Kuya Nate, the main artist and the head of the project, together with Kevin, Jinju, and AstroKikko. Batang ETH draws inspiration from the great artwork of Azuki and aims to showcase Filipino culture within the generated NFT. Generation 1 supply consists of 5000 generative Batang ETH NFT. The main goal of this project is to help the children dealing with cancer in the Philippines. This project aims to give these children help, hope, and heart to live.

Mission

“Create and Craft"

To discover, develop, and deliver innovative art that helps artists hone their knowledge and skill. We also aim to provide knowledge and bridge them into the Metaverse.

Vision

“Connect and Empowerment”

Our vision is a world where local artists and NFT collectors come together to support and build rapport with each other. We have the power to further develop and introduce Filipino art to the world.
